    Season 9 of The Masked Singer has been nothing short of incredible. With some of the biggest star reveals ever and some truly impressive talent, we have high expectations going into New York Night. And one of the characters will definitely be bringing cool vibes…the Polar Bear!

    But who could be under the mask? We’ll break down everything we know so far.

    Clues on The Masked Singer Season 9

    Unlike past seasons, we don’t have any clues ahead of Polar Bear’s appearance on the show! But when we did get clues, here’s what we found out. Polar Bear started by saying he’s excited to drop some “New York flavor” on the stage, mentioning it’s where he comes from. We see a mailbox with flowers blooming out of it. He says his neighborhood has a bad rap, then holds up a scratch-off lottery ticket that says “COLD HARD CASH” on it.

    Polar Bear says he developed an interest in electronics as a kid. He’s seen working on a toaster and hair dryer. Eventually this sparked a “knockout” idea, he says while punching a punching bag. He “turned the tables on the entire music industry.” Polar Bear invented a sound no one was making! The camera then pans to a message in a bottle. Now they’re here to honor a fellow New Yorker!

    After his performance, we got a clue from the Long Island Medium herself, Theresa Caputo. She shared that she saw him giving an acceptance speech at the Rock N Roll Hall of Fame!

    Popular Guesses for The Masked Singer Polar Bear

    Robin Thicke predicted this is DJ Jazzy Jeff, but Nick Cannon quickly pointed out that he’s from Philly. Ken thought it might be P Diddy, even though he got boo’ed by the crowd for his guess. Jenny went another direction, saying it could be Flava Flav. Finally, Nicole guessed the “OG,” Grandmaster Flash.

    Fans online largely agreed with Nicole’s Grandmaster Flash prediction. Other popular guesses were LL Cool J, Marlon Wayans, Ray J, and Tracy Morgan.

    Who is the Polar Bear on The Masked Singer?

    Polar Bear was the first to be unmasked on New York Night! And he was revealed to be…

    Grandmaster Flash!

    Another unbelievable reveal for season 9. This has truly been the most STACKED cast we’ve seen yet…and that’s not even mentioning California Roll and Medusa, who haven’t been unmasked yet.
