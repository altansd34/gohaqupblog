High vs low fantasy: what do they mean and what is the difference?

13 Aug 2024, 00:00

The setting of the world in which a story takes place is one of the fundamental elements that differentiate high and low fantasy. The latter is characterized by stories that take place in an ordinary world, albeit with some aspects of fantasy. High fantasy, on the other hand, is characterized by a setting that involves an alternative world that is purely the creation of the storyteller. There are numerous sub-types of the fantasy genre in novels, video games, films, and television shows.
