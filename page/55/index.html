Gas prices on Monday dipped below $2 a gallon for the first time since March 2009, according to AAA. The national average for a gallon of regular gasoline today is $1.998, according to AAA’s daily fuel gauge, which surveys as many as 120,000 gas stations. Prices are down almost 40 cents from last year. “Drivers across the country are celebrating the historic return of cheaper gas prices,” Marshall Doney, president and chief executive of AAA, said in a statement.
