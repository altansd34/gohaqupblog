    It’s officially been teased that Derek Hough is interested in an “all winners” season of Dancing With the Stars. Although the odds of it being season 32 are unlikely (due to Ariana Madix’s Season 32 confirmation), let’s look back at all of the past winners of Dancing With the Stars.

    Everything to Know About Dancing With the Stars Season 32

    Dancing With the Stars is back this fall with a few changes. Although every detail about the upcoming season is currently unknown, here are a few things that have been confirmed.

    Tyra Banks has officially left the series, and former pro dancer Julianne Hough will replace her as Alfonso Ribeiro’s cohost. The judging panel will feature three judges this season, Derek Hough, Bruno Tonioli, and Carrie Ann Inaba. In turn, the show hopes to keep Len Goodman‘s legacy alive through their upcoming weeks of the competition.

    While discussing ways that the cast hopes to keep Goodman’s legacy alive, Derek Hough admitted that he’s interested in an ‘all winners’ season of the hit show. During this, he specifically named dropped his former partners, Bindi Irwin and Nicole Scherzinger.

    This interview has peaked the interest in fans, so let’s look back at who all of the winners have been. Maybe if we’re lucky, we’ll see the majority of this list in an upcoming season. Here’s each winner of Dancing With the Stars, seasons 1 through 31.

    Each Celebrity, Pro Winner of Dancing With the Stars Ever

    1. Kelly Monaco, Alec Mazo

    In 2005, General Hospital actress Kelly Monaco won with pro Alec Mazo.

    2. Drew Lachey, Cheryl Burke

    In spring 2006, singer and actor Drew Lachey won with pro Cheryl Burke.

    3. Emmitt Smith, Cheryl Burke

    In fall 2006, former NFL player Emmitt Smith won with pro Cheryl Burke.

    4. Apolo Anton Ohno, Julianne Hough

    In spring 2007, Olympic speed skater Apolo Anton Ohno won with pro Julianne Hough.

    5. Hélio Castroneves, Julianne Hough

    In fall 2007, Indy 500 driver Hélio Castroneves won with pro Julianne Hough.

    6. Kristi Yamaguchi, Mark Ballas

    In spring 2008, Olympic figure skater Kristi Yamaguchi won with pro Mark Ballas.

    7. Brooke Burke, Derek Hough

    In fall 2008, TV host and model Brooke Burke won with pro Derek Hough.

    8. Shawn Johnson, Mark Ballas

    In spring 2009, Olympic gymnast Shawn Johnson won with pro Mark Ballas.

    9. Donny Osmond, Kym Johnson

    In fall 2009, actor and singer Donny Osmond won with pro Kym Johnson.

    10. Nicole Scherzinger, Derek Hough

    In spring 2010, singer-songwriter Nicole Scherzinger won with pro Derek Hough.

    11. Jennifer Gray, Derek Hough

    In fall 2010, Dirty Dancing actress Jennifer Gray won with pro Derek Hough.

    12. Hines Ward, Kym Johnson

    In spring 2011, former NFL wide receiver Hines Ward won with pro Kym Johnson.

    13. J.R. Martinez, Karina Smirnoff

    In fall 2011, actor and army veteran J.R. Martinez won with pro Karina Smirnoff.

    14. Donald Driver, Peta Murgatroyd

    In spring 2012, former NFL wide receiver Donald Driver won with pro Peta Murgatroyd.

    15 Melissa Rycroft, Tony Dovolani

    In fall 2012, reality star Melissa Rycroft won the first ever ‘All Stars’ season with pro Tony Dovolani. Ahead of season 15, she placed third on season 8.

    16. Kellie Pickler, Derek Hough

    In spring 2013, country singer Kellie Pickler won with pro Derek Hough.

    17. Amber Riley, Derek Hough

    In fall 2013, Glee actress Amber Riley won with pro Derek Hough.

    18. Meryl Davis, Maksim Chmerkovskiy

    In spring 2014, Olympic ice dancer Meryl Davis won with pro Maksim Chmerkovskiy.

    19. Alfonso Ribeiro, Witney Carson

    In spring 2014, Fresh Prince of Bel-Air actor Alfonso Ribeiro won with pro Witney Carson.

    20. Rumer Willis, Valentin Chmerkovskiy

    In spring 2015, actress Rumer Willis won with pro Valentin Chmerkovskiy.

    21. Bindi Irwin, Derek Hough

    In fall 2015, wildlife conservationist, presenter and actress Bindi Irwin won with pro Derek Hough.

    22. Nyle DiMarco, Peta Murgatroyd

    In spring 2016, actor, model and spokesman Nyle DiMarco won with pro Peta Murgatroyd.

    23. Laurie Hernandez, Valentin Chmerkovskiy

    In fall 2016, Olympic gymnast Laurie Hernandez won with pro Valentin Chmerkovskiy.

    24. Rashad Jennings, Emma Slater

    In spring 2017, NFL running back Rashad Jennings won with pro Emma Slater.

    25. Jordan Fisher, Lindsay Arnold

    In fall 2017, actor, singer and dancer Jordan Fisher won with pro Lindsay Arnold.

    26. Adam Rippon and Jenna Johnson

    In spring 2018, Olympic figure skater Adam Rippon won the first ever ‘Athletes Edition’ of the show. He won with pro Jenna Johnson.

    27. Bobby Bones, Sharna Burgess

    In fall 2018, radio personality Bobby Bones won with pro Sharna Burgess.

    28. Hannah Brown, Alan Bersten

    In fall 2019, Bachelorette Star Hannah Brown won with pro Alan Bersten.

    29. Kaitlyn Bristowe, Artem Chigvintsev

    In fall 2020, Bachelorette Star Kaitlyn Bristowe won with pro Artem Chigvintsev.

    30. Iman Shumpert, Daniella Karagach

    In fall 2021, former NBA player Iman Shumpert won with pro Daniella Karagach.

    31. Charli D’Amelio, Mark Ballas

    In fall 2022, social media personality Charli D’Amelio won with pro Mark Ballas.
