    Breaking: Nigerian Air Force releases list of successful applicants for short service

    The Nigerian Air Force (NAF) in the evening of Thursday, January 17, released the list of successful candidates shortlisted for the 2018 direct short service commission cadet training asking them to report in Kaduna for the exercise.

    07 Aug 2024, 00:00

    The Nigerian Air Force (NAF) in the evening of Thursday, January 17, released the list of successful candidates shortlisted for the 2018 direct short service commission cadet training asking them to report in Kaduna for the exercise.

    The NAF asked the shortlisted candidates to report or training at the Military Training Centre, Nigerian Air Base, Kaduna on Monday, January 21, 2019 with materials which it listed too.

    A statement confirming the list was signed by Air Vice Marshal Samuel Babalola on behalf of the Chief of the Air Staff (CAS).

    Below is the full statement from the NAF and the list of the shortlisted candidates:

    SPECIAL INSTRUCTIONS

    1. The underlisted candidates were successful at the Nigerian Air Force Direct Short Service Commission (DSSC) Selection Board held at the Nigerian Air Force Base, Kaduna.

    2. Candidates are to report for Cadet Training at Military Training Centre, Nigerian Air Base, Kaduna on Monday 21 January 2019. Candidates are to come along with the following:

    a. Original and Photocopy of Credentials.
