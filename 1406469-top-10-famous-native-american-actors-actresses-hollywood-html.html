    Native American actors and actresses have been represented in the motion picture industry since the early days of Hollywood.     6. Tantoo Cardinal

    • Name: Tantoo Cardinal
    • Date of birth: July 20 1950
    • Place of birth: Anzac, Fort McMurray, Alberta, Canada
    • Age: 70 years (as of 2021)
    • Occupation: Actress

    Tantoo Cardinal started acting in part to change the public perceptions of the Native Americans.

    She is best known for her roles in films such as Dances With Wolves (1990), Legends of the Fall (1994), and Smoke Signals (1998), as well as the television shows like Dr. Quinn and Medicine Woman.

    Cardinal continues her political activism today. In August 2011, she and actress Margot Kidder were arrested during an environmental protest at the White House.
