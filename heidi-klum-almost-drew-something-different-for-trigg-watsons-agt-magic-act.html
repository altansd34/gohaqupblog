    On Tuesday night, magician Trigg Watson performed during the latest America’s Got Talent live show. He enlisted judge Heidi Klum for help, asking her to draw a moment in her life. Klum chose to draw her wedding, but she almost drew something very different.

    Heidi Klum Talks About Trigg Watson’s AGT Act

    During Trigg’s latest performance, he gave Klum a piece of paper and a pen, asking her to draw “a moment in which you felt a strong connection to someone special.” He told her it didn’t “have to be fancy,” but she should autograph it when she was done.

    Klum ended up drawing her wedding to husband Tom Kaulitz. Trigg took the drawing and magically passed it to his friend Kyle, who was appearing via video chat. Kyle hung it on his wall. Klum was clearly impressed by the trick.

    However, she revealed to People that she was originally thinking of drawing childbirth, explaining, “In that moment I was like, ‘What are your special moments?’ I immediately thought birth. And then I was thinking immediately, ‘Oh my God, I can’t paint that!'”

    It’s not clear if Trigg’s act would have been affected if Klum had drawn something different. As it was, the magician was impressed by Klum’s art, saying, “That drawing of her and her husband in 15 seconds was amazing.”

    Will Trigg Make It to the Finals?

    On Wednesday night’s results show, we’ll find out which two acts from this week’s episode of AGT made it into the Season 18 Finals. Did Trigg earn enough votes from the viewers at home to perform again in the finale?

    Trigg’s latest performance received mixed reactions from the judges. Simon Cowell told the magician that he thinks his “presentation is a bit annoying.” This comment earned boos from the audience. Meanwhile, Howie Mandel said the act got “a little boring” in the middle.

    However, Klum and Sofia Vergara were impressed by the act, with Klum calling it “pretty special” and Vergara saying it was something she’s “never seen before on this stage.”
