    Sofia Vergara has everyone’s hearts racing every Thursday when she posts throwback pictures to her modeling days. This week was no different. The 49-year-old posted a sexy bikini photo that has fans flocking to the comments section on Instagram to praise the Colombian beauty.

    Sofia Vergara Stuns In Sexy Throwback Bikini Photo

    In the photo, Sofia is wearing a tiny black bikini that accentuates her toned body. The photo was taken in the ’90s in Miami, Florida. Sofia still had her natural blond locks on display and has since dyed her hair darker after becoming a television star. The ocean serves as the backdrop of this gorgeous photo.

    The photo has over 271,000 likes on Instagram. The comments section is full of heart-eye emojis and love for the ‘AGT’ judge who is currently gearing up to judge the 16th season of the show. “Wow queen u haven’t aged a day!” wrote one fan. “Love your young photos,” wrote another.

    SEE ALSO: 50 SEXIEST SOFIA VERGARA BIKINI PICTURES FROM THE ’80S TO NOW — WAS SHE SEXIER THEN OR NOW?

    Modeling has clearly been in Sofia’s blood from the start of her career. She shared another throwback picture flashing a pose for the cameras when she was little living in Barranquilla, Colombia in the ’80s. She never initially had plans to pursue a career in modeling. Instead, she wanted to be a dentist.

    When she was 17, she was approached by a photographer during a family outing. She initially turned down his offer because of her Catholic faith, but she later received permission from the strict sisters at her Catholic school to pursue modeling. Soon after she shot a commercial for Pepsi which became a huge success. This was the start of a massive career for Sofia which has led to her being the world’s highest-paid actress and joining ‘AGT.’

    If you want to know more about how Sofia got her start at modeling, check out the What’s Hot Video below. It delves into 10 facts about her and you may learn things you never knew.

    Sofia Is Back As A Talent Show Judge For An Exciting New Season

    Sofia and the other ‘AGT’ judges participated in a video encouraging people to try out for ‘AGT.’ The last day to submit a virtual audition for a chance to be a part of Season 16 is on March 6. Howie Mandel, Heidi Klum and Sofia had to hold it down last season after Simon Cowell broke his back. This led to him missing the live shows on ‘AGT.’ She provided comedic relief on the judging panel, not to mention her outfits were all incredible.
