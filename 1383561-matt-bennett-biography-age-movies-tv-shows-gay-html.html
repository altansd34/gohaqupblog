    Matthew Hank Bennett, commonly known by his stage name Matt Bennett, is a sensational American-born actor, singer, and writer. He became a household name after his role as Robbie Shapiro in the Nickelodeon sitcom known as Victorious.

    Matt made his television debut in 2009 with the role of Jamie in the TV short Totally for Teens. In 2011, he was nominated for a Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Award (UK) in the category of Nick UK's Funniest Person.

    Biography

    Matt Bennett was born on November 13, 1991, in Massapequa, New York, USA. He grew up in Long Island, New York alongside his sister, Ally Bennett. His parents divorced when he was still young.

    Education

    He studied at Massapequa High school and graduated in 2008. While in high school, he did not consider building his career as an actor since he wanted to become an inventor. He was an excellent performer who scored high grades throughout his high school studies.

    Acting career

    The actor, popularly known as Robbie from Victorious, began his career by featuring in commercials, thus triggering his interest in the entertainment industry.

    He joined the Way Off Broadway company located in Long Island. Matt also joined an American improvisational theatre and training centre known as the Upright Citizens Brigade Theater (UCB).

    Eventually, he relocated to Los Angeles, California and started getting more offers for commercial voice acting. One of his first roles was as Jamie in the 2009 TV short Totally for Teens.

    Matt has taken part in many other numerous other films, TV shows, and web series.
