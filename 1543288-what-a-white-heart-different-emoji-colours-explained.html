    Sending plain texts makes a conversation dull, and if you want to make it more engaging, you can include emojis. A heart emoji is the most commonly used, with multiple colours representing different emotions. If you choose to use the white one, it is essential to understand what it means and when to use it. What does a white heart mean, and what are the meanings of other coloured heart emojis?

    What does a white heart mean?

    What does the white heart emoji mean? Among the many coloured heart emojis, the white one is unique. It has been around since 2019 and is available on social media platforms, including WhatsApp, Twitter, and Instagram. The symbol may represent:

    • Pure love – The emoji can show pure love between a parent and their child or romantic partners. The symbol signifies how valuable someone is in your life, and you can include it in a special text you send them.
    • Emotional support – If someone is going through a difficult phase in their life, sending a white love emoji shows you are in solidarity with them. It is a reassurance of your support in their difficult times.
    • A special bond – Close bonds with friends, relatives, and sometimes, workmates can be expressed using the white emoji. It symbolises a tight bond of great friendship and deep family love.

    What does it mean if someone sends you a white heart emoji?

    You might feel slightly confused when you receive a white heart emoji from someone on social media. To interpret the emoji’s meaning correctly, you need to assess your relationship with the person and know the context in which it is sent. The sender might be your crush, family member, workmate, or friend and then try to establish why they sent you the symbol.

    Suppose you receive the symbol from a guy who is your crush; then you should interpret it as an expression of love. It may take up different meanings depending on your relationship with the guy.

    How do you respond to a white heart emoji?

    It is always good to respond to a message when you receive it. For a message with a white heart emoji, you can respond by sending a similar one or sending a different coloured heart emoji. Interestingly, there are numerous coloured heart emojis with different meanings you can use.

    Heart emoji colour meanings

    Heart emojis have existed for a long time, with the red one being the most popular. However, not all of them have the same meanings and use. Here are the different heart emojis with their meanings and usage.

    1. Red

    The red one depicts love. The symbol can be used to express affection between romantically involved people. Close friends can also use it to show their love for one another.

    2. Blue

    Blue is a cool colour; its emoji symbolises trust, loyalty, and peace. This symbol is best used in conversation with friends or a person with whom you are in a platonic relationship.

    3. Purple

    This emoji signifies sensitivity and compassionate love. People who love things with purple colours, such as dresses or shoes, may share pictures of the items on social accompanied by the symbol.

    4. Black

    The symbol may mean a dark humour or a dark, twisted soul. The emoji has been used to convey different messages, including a dark joke, a sombre mood, and support for everyone. In recent years, the symbol has been used to show support for the movement Black Lives Matter.

    5. Yellow

    It means liking and friendship, as opposed to romance, depicted by most heart emojis. The emoji can be used to show happiness, positivity, and hope. You can send it to people who are dear to you and you are not romantically involved with them.

    6. Green

    The coloured heart means support, optimism, close connections, and adoration of green things. It evokes caring and platonic love feelings, usually between friends or close partners in an activity such as work. The symbol may also be used to show love for the environment.

    7. Pink

    The emoji symbolises sincerity. It may mean strong happiness, passion, excitement, or obsession with something to other people. It is most commonly used by family members expressing genuine care or friends who have achieved a new level of friendship. You can also use it to show your liking of things with pink colour.

    8. Orange

    The orange heart emoji means vibrancy, enthusiasm, and joy. Since the colour expresses warmth and light, you can send it to friends to show support, encouragement, and positivity in whatever situation they may be in.

    9. Brown

    It means an expression of love, affection, and close ties. The symbol is most used to show support for racial equality and justice for people of colour.
