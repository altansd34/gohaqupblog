    Nicole Scherzinger, the renowned Pussycat Dolls singer, is unquestionably worth the fortune she has amassed over the past two decades. A world-famous singer and talent show judge, Scherzinger has built up a star’s reputation that puts her name up as a high-profile artist. We explore what her net worth actually is and how much does she actually make?

    Scherzinger Had Diverse Ways of Finding Success

    Scherzinger is a great example of a celebrity that finds success in every opportunity. On that note, the singer has made multiple attempts at making it big in the entertainment world and her story is a great inspiration to fans all over.

    She started out as a member of The Pussycat Dolls, a dance troupe that turned into a pop group. The Pussycat Dolls is the main driving force that helped put Scherzinger’s name into the spotlight. The group sold more than 55 million records worldwide and it’s speculated that each member earned six figures a year.

    Scherzinger also booked several TV show gigs, most notably her stint as a judge on The X Factor. She then became the highest-paid judge on the show, Scherzinger’s was reportedly being paid approximately $2.5 million for a single season. Scherzinger also became part of the panelists on The Masked Singer in 2019 and although no salary was disclosed, it surely helped in increasing her fortune.

    Scherzinger’s Actual Net Worth

    Now that we’ve touched upon how much The Pussycat Dolls’ lead singer makes, let’s take a look at her net worth. Various sources show that Scherzinger’s net worth is $14 million which was made during her 20 years in entertainment.

    As to what she spends her money on, Scherzinger once paid $3.75 million for a property in the Hollywood Hills. The four-story home features extravagant amenities such as an outdoor spa and a wine cellar. Aside from that, she also purchased a home from Daisy Fuentes paying $2.16 million. Both properties are listed as for sale and are priced reasonably enough for the singer to make a decent profit.

    It’s not a stretch to say that Scherzinger is deserving of every penny that she earned from the showbiz industry.
