    Country stars Lauren Alaina and Blake Shelton faced off in an epic beer pong battle on the Season 2 finale of Shelton’s outrageous game show Barmageddon. Alaina completely dominated the typically unflappable Shelton in the alcohol-fueled challenge.

    Shelton No Match for Alaina in Outsized Beer Pong

    The Barmageddon finale features Lauren Alaina as the celebrity guest. Shelton’s longtime friend and former tourmate enthusiastically took on the host in a series of bar-themed games with over-the-top twists.

    Alongside Shelton and co-host Carson Daly, Alaina helps close out a raucous sophomore season filled with raunchy hijinks. Past guests like Kelly Clarkson, Ice-T, and Michelle Rodriguez added to the hilarious debauchery throughout Season 2’s themed episodes.

    In a clip, Shelton and Alaina face off in Barmageddon‘s giant take on beer pong. Rather than simple ping pong balls and cups, they fling oversized balls towards each other’s body-length receptacles.

    Shelton struggled hilariously with his unwieldy paddle, barely landing any shots. An openly mocking Alaina quipped “This is your hero?” at the country star’s poor performance. Her superior hand-eye coordination was certainly on full display.

    The trash-talking Alaina then easily dominated a flustered Shelton, who had no comeback against her beer pong mastery. She left the usually unflappable star both humbled and speechless.

    Finale Caps Absurd Sophomore Season of Barmageddon

    Barmageddon‘s second season has delivered nonstop outrageous fun since premiering last fall. Shelton and Daly conceived the show as a real-life version of The Hangover movies.

    With its over-the-top concept powered by Shelton’s persona, Barmageddon encapsulates today’s escapist appetite for irreverent humor. The larger-than-life finale aims to end the season on the highest of high notes.

    Each week, celebrities join Shelton at his Nashville bar Ole Red for games amped up with alcohol-fueled twists. The Alaina-Shelton beer pong showdown exemplifies Barmageddon‘s spirit of silliness and celebrity mischief. Their country music rapport heightens the hilarity.

    Barmageddon‘s foundation lies in Shelton and Daly’s real-life friendship. Their natural comedic chemistry makes the chaotic proceedings unexpectedly endearing. Underneath the raunchy exterior, an earnest camaraderie emerges between the duo. Their obvious history of laughter and good-natured ribbing infuses all the games with heart.

    Lauren Alaina’s appearance lets her join in on that genuine fun. Seeing Shelton temporarily trounced at his own game only expands the hilarity.
