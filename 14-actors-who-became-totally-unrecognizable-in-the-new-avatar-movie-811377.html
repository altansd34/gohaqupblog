    The audience had to wait for the release of the new Avatar movie for a long 13 years. The main characters of the film are the people of Na’vi who live on the planet Pandora. And they look super-realistic thanks to motion capture technology and the famous actors who play them. We decided to compare the photos of real actors and actresses and their characters in the movie Avatar: The Way of Water, and we were struck by how different they look (though some facial features are still recognizable).

    1. Sam Worthington as Jake Sully, a former human, who became the chief of the Omatikaya
