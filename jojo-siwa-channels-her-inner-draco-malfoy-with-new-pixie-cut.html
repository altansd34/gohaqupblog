    JoJo Siwa recently surprised fans with the arrival of her Halloween costume. Her new hairdo works perfectly while channeling her inner Draco Malfoy and fans are impressed with how much she looks like the Harry Potter character.

    Siwa’s New Hairstyle Helps Her Look Like Draco Malfoy

    JoJo Siwa has completely transformed into the iconic villain from the Harry Potter franchise. Her looks is complete with the green and black robe, as well as a slick blonde hairstyle. Clearly, Siwa could pass as an identical copy of Malfoy. Since premiering the look, she has created a series of herself lip-syncing Malfoy’s famous lines from Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone.

    “This is Crabbe, and Goyle,” Siwa lip-synced in the video. “And I’m Malfoy. Draco Malfoy.”

    The Dancing With The Stars runner-up mimicked every scowl and snarl of the character she’s cosplaying, including the smug hostility of Malfoy. The caption of the video shows three snake emojis, paying tribute to Slytherin’s house mascot. 

    Fans are currently loving Siwa’s costume, since the accuracy is spot on. With Halloween on the rise, many are wondering if this will be the dancers only costume of the season. Nonetheless, she totally understood the assignment.

    Siwa Impressed Her Girlfriend

    Fans aren’t the only ones impressed with Siwa’s cosplaying. Her new girlfriend, Avery Cyrus, showed her support on the app. Being a TikTok star herself, Cyrus wrote a comment that expresses her support and love for Siwa.

    “All the sudden I have a crush on Draco Malfoy,” Cyrus commented.

    The couple went public with their relationship shortly after Siwa’s former girlfriend Kylie Prew revealed that they broke up a few months back. They started dating after Cyrus graffitied a white bedsheet, spelling out the message: “JoJo be my GF?” To which Siwa answered with a “yes,” starting their relationship together.

    Siwa frequently documents her daily life on her Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube pages at the same time adding her own quirkiness to each video.
