    America’s Got Talent Season 18 returns on Tuesday night with the second live show of the season, as 11 more acts take the stage for America’s vote. Only two acts will make it to the finale during Wednesday’s results show.

    AGT has already shared which acts will be performing on this week’s episode, airing on NBC. The list includes not one but two Golden Buzzer acts. Read on to find out who this week’s acts are and revisit their impressive auditions from earlier in the season.

    Who’s Performing on AGT Tonight?

    Ahren Belisle

    This comedian, who has cerebral palsy, uses a text-to-speech app to tell hilarious jokes. His audition made the judges laugh and earned him a standing ovation. Now Ahren is taking his great sense of humor to the Season 18 live shows.

    Alfie Andrew

    This 12-year-old singer from the UK stunned the AGT audience with his performance of “Hold My Hand” by Lady Gaga during the audition round. The judges chose to send the young talent through to the live shows.

    Andrew Stanton

    This performer totally freaked out the judges with his dangerous audition, which involved sword-swallowing like we’ve never seen it before. Despite being disturbed by the act, the judges chose to send Andrew to the live shows.

    Atlanta Drum Academy

    This energetic drumline, led by the young Chioma, impressed host Terry Crews so much that he chose to hit the Golden Buzzer and send them straight to the live shows. Now they’ll compete for a shot at the Season 18 finale.

    Dani Kerr

    Judge Simon Cowell stopped this singer in the middle of her audition and asked her to sing a different song. She ended up performing an original track called “November.” The judges were impressed enough to send her to the live shows.

    Erica Coffelt

    This dancer brought plenty of energy and charisma to the stage during auditions. She made the judges smile, and they chose to send Erica through to the live shows. Now she’s fighting for a spot in the Season 18 finale.

    Murmuration

    This French dance group mesmerized the judges and audience with their unique routine during the auditions. Judge Howie Mandel was so impressed that he hit the Golden Buzzer to send Murmuration straight to the live shows.

    Ryland Petty

    This 10-year-old magician from the UK involved the judges in his act during the auditions. His magic was so impressive that the judges chose to send him to the live shows and give him an opportunity to earn America’s vote.

    Sharpe Family Singers

    This musical family charmed the crowd with their harmonies on a cover of “How Far I’ll Go” from the Disney movie Moana. It made the judges smile, and earned them a spot in the live shows for a chance at receiving America’s vote.

    Steel Panther

    This rock band totally owned the stage with their performance of “Eyes of a Tiger” during the audition round. Now they’re taking their nostalgic energy to the live shows for a chance to make it into the Season 18 finale.

    Three G

    This trio of acrobats from Ukraine had a couple of missteps during their audition, but the judges admired their perseverance and chose to send them to the live shows. Now they’re fighting for a chance to compete in the finale.
