    Dancing With the Stars professional Sharna Burgess and her boyfriend Brian Austin Green recently celebrated a huge milestone. The couple was seen publicly celebrating two years of knowing one another. Green shared family photos on his Instagram showing the wonderful life they have together with their children.

    Brian Austin Green Posts Adorable Family Photos For Two-Year Anniversary of Meeting Sharna Burgess

    The definition of a perfect family is clear in the latest Instagram pictures of Green, only made more special by the message that came along with it. The message revealed that Green met Burgess two years prior for coffee.

    “October 16th 2 years ago was a day like any other day, but I made plans to meet someone new for coffee. My god am I thankful every single day for that,” he wrote in the caption. “You are a light in my life, our kids lives, and the most amazing person I have ever met.”

    Burgess replied in the comments, stating that her dreams came true on that day as well. It’s clear that this couple is just as in love as they were when they competed on Dancing With the Stars.

    Along with the couple in the photos are Green’s three children with Megan Fox, Noah (10), Bodhi (8), and Journey (6). Green and Burgess’s three month old son Zane also appears in the photos. The family’s love for one another is shown in the photos.

    Burgess made her own anniversary post sharing a video showing Green with their baby a day before the event. She stated in the caption that her boyfriend keeps getting hotter, showing throwback photos of Green.

    The pair met in a coffee shop back in 2020, shortly after the actor broke up with Fox following nearly ten years of marriage. Both have high-profile careers, and a mutual business manager that set the two up for a coffee. After a few dates, they announced their relationship in January 2021 and later competed together in season 30 of DWTS.

    Will Burgess Return To Dancing With The Stars?

    As the professional dancer sits out season 31 of DWTS and is now occupied with being a full-time mom for her children, fans are asking if it’s possible for a return to the show. Burgess has competed on DWTS for 15 straight seasons and has decided to not return for the latest season.

    Taking a step back to focus on motherhood, Burgess expressed the importance of being there for her child. Between practice and actual show days, and the travel time from work to home, Burgess stated that it’s too much time away from the family.

    “I’m going to live and love every single bit of it. But it does not mean that I won’t be around,” Burgess said. “You will see me, but it will just be in a different way.”

    Her love of dance will have to wait as she reveals she won’t be competing in the near future. Instead, she claims that she’ll be involved in the show in some way. Burgess can return as a guest judge or a dance choreographer, only time will tell what her plans are for the show.
