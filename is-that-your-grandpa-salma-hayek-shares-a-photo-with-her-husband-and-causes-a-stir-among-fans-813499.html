    19 Aug 2024, 00:00

    “You only married him for the money,” one user wrote. Salma Hayek is head over heels with her husband, the French billionaire Francois-Henri Pinault, who she met in 2006 at the opening of the Palazzo Grassi exhibition in Venice. They were both smitten with each other and since then, their love has been stronger than ever. However, one innocent Happy Birthday post on Instagram caused a major stir among fans.

    Their love started with a “silly” conversation.

    AFP/EAST NEWS

    The couple met in 2006 and didn’t even know that their chit-chat would flourish into something serious. Salma recollects, “It was so silly. We started talking about football. We’re both crazy about soccer. Then we started talking about science. We love space and physics, and he was so smart, charming, and funny.” Only later did she realize that Francois-Henri was her perfect match.

    They have a big family.

    Salma and Francois-Henri have 4 kids — she’s a stepmom to 3 kids from her husband’s previous relationship. The actress believes she got blessed with them. Hayek also has one biological child, Valentina, who she gave birth to at the age of 41. The star opens up, “I always wanted to have many children, and I wasn’t able to. My body, as a miracle, had one.”

    Salma shared an intimate photo on Instagram.

    Hayek’s husband turned 61 on Sunday, and decided to take the opportunity and express her love for her billionaire husband on Instagram. Salma wraps her arms around Francois-Henri in the photo and lovingly plants a smooch on his cheek. Salma also left a heartwarming message, “Happy birthday, mi amor. You’re my sunshine, my warmth, my light, my strength, my joy, my love.”

    The post received a lot of criticism.

    Stuart Hardy/ABACAPRESS.COM/Abaca/East News

    Not all fans were excited about the lovely message Salma wanted to share with the world. Some pointed out that the difference in appearance between Salma and Francois-Henri was noticeable. Someone even dared to ask, “Is that your grandpa?” and “Is that her dad?”. Others claimed that she married only for the billionaire’s money. Luckily, the majority of fans were happy for the couple. Someone wrote, “Happy birthday! You look wonderful together!”

    The age gap is actually not that big.

    It might seem like Francois-Henri is much older than Salma, yet, in fact, the difference is only 4 years — Salma is 56 and was born in 1966, while her husband is 61 and was born in 1962. So he’s neither her “dad” nor “grandpa.”

    Salma does know about the rumors.

    LAURENT BENHAMOU/SIPA/EAST NEWS

    The star is aware of the criticism surrounding her marriage. She says, “I didn’t know who he was. I just heard or maybe read in a headline that you had married a very rich guy. I meet him, and I’m like, ‘this guy is so foxy, the confidence, his eyes, he’s so good-looking and charming.’ When I married him, everybody said, ‘It’s an arranged marriage, she’s marrying him for the money’ I’m like, ‘Whatever, think what you want.’ I don’t even get offended.”

    You don’t have to have millions to look good, even Salma herself proves that. She rarely works out and just makes sure to be as active as possible during the day. Simple swimming and walks are her favorite pastime.
