    The Masked Singer is back tonight with an all new group of celebrities! Now that the Turtle, Kangaroo, and White Tiger have already secured their spots in the Super 9, who will the next three be?

    Here are the six NEW costumed celebrities we’ll see perform tonight, plus a sneak peek at a performance!

    Sneak Peek at Kitty on The Masked Singer Group B

    The first performance teased from Group B, the Kitty is bringing it on The Masked Singer stage. She has confidence and the voice to back it up. The sequin and feathered covered “showgirl” costume just screams performer! I love the two different colored eyes too. Kitty is a contender to go far! Could she be someone from a girl group? Or a solo performer looking to make her mark?

    LAST WEEK’S RECAP OF THE MASKED SINGER GROUP A CHAMPIONSHIPS + OUR KANGAROO PREDICTION

    Who is the Banana?

    One of the goofier costumes this season, the Banana definitely has personality! The Masked Singer already teased his entrance and it is full of energy. He gives me strong comedian vibes, but it really could be anyone at this point. A lot of people online are predicting it may be Howie Mandel from America’s Got Talent! Now that’s a crossover we could get behind.

    Who is the Frog?

    The Frog is dressed in his Sunday best, a purple suit with a yellow patterned tie and a hat with a yellow feather in it. We haven’t seen much from him so far (except for some sweet dance moves for about half a second), so this one is really up in the air. I love his facial expression though!

    IS THE TURTLE THIS FAMOUS BOY BAND MEMBER? HERE’S WHAT THE CLUES SAY!

    Who is the Taco?

    I have said this before and I will say it again, the Taco is the creepiest costume on all three seasons of The Masked Singer so far. Not just in Group B, but in seasons 1-3, in any group. Why does he have to have a tomato for a face? This can’t be someone who takes themselves too seriously. I mean, come on…look at that costume. And please vote it out tonight.

    Who is the Elephant?

    On the other hand, Elephant has one of the most meta costumes we’ve seen. It’s like a dark version of the first season’s Poodle. The costume also makes it seem like this might be a pretty big person, but the fact that it’s an Elephant could be making it look that way. This was one of the costumes I was most excited about when it was announced, so I can’t wait to see Elephant in action.

    Who is the Mouse?

    Now if the clues line up in the teaser video for season 3, the identity of the Mouse might already be known. But considering the Robot had cowboy clues and it was Lil Wayne, we’ll guess that those clues were just to throw us off. The Mouse is dressed in a flower crown and a wedding dress, hiding a curvy body underneath. Will she become the Kitty’s arch-nemesis though? Only time will tell if she even has a chance.

    OUR PRE-SEASON PREDICTION FOR THE MOUSE AHEAD OF HER GROUP B PERFORMANCE

    Tune in to The Masked Singer tonight on FOX at 8/7c for all these performers, and the first Group B unmasking!
