    The Jonas Brothers recently announced the launch of a new mini-series Moments Between The Moments. Their latest project comes fresh off the heels of the Jonas Brothers Family Roast on Netflix and is set to give viewers an inside look into intimate moments during their 2021 tour “Remember This.”

    The pop trio have been inseparable ever since reuniting in February of 2019. Kevin, Joe, and Nick released their comeback album “Happiness Begins” and embarked on the”Happiness Begins” tour in summer 2019. The successful concert tour put the global superstars back in the hearts and minds of adoring fans worldwide.

    “As some of you already know we were doing a bit of filming during the #RememberThisTour. Starting tomorrow we’re dropping a mini-series (Including footage from a special secret show in LA) all month long on Facebook,” the group announced on Instagram on Monday.

    In the promo, the brothers wear Boston Red Sox jerseys. This may hint that the first episode, “A Year Of Firsts,” will give us a look into their iconic show in Fenway Park last October.

    This series will air throughout the month on Facebook and Instagram. The episodes will include behind the scenes moments between the brothers off-stage that they shared made “these last few months special.”

    The brothers are no strangers to opening up to their fans in intimate ways. Their Amazon Prime documentary “Chasing Happiness” revealed the emotional reason behind their split.

    “There were moments I thought they would never speak to me again,” former The Voice judge Nick Jonas said at one point in the Amazon original.

    Between their electrifying tours and the energy the brothers show on social media, perhaps the long break was needed to fix a strained relationship. With their latest single “Who’s In Your Head” nearing 10 million views on YouTube, there is no telling what is next for the three brothers.
